Digital Banking Step-by-Steps

Online & Mobile Banking Help

Skip the drive and take control of your banking anywhere, any time. Learn how to manage your account, transfers, ePayments, Bill Pay and more from your phone or computer - it's a smarter, more convenient way to bank.

Account Basics — Desktop

  • How do I find my online banking username on a desktop?
    1. Open Online Banking on desktop
    2. Choose Forgot Username
    3. Choose I want to...
      1. Verify your identify
      2. Select the delivery method for your One-Time Code
      3.  Enter the Verification Code
    4. View your username

  • How do I find my online banking password on a desktop?
    1. Open Online Banking on desktop
    2. Choose Forgot Password
    3. Choose I want to...
      1. Verify your Identify, enter username, SSN, DOB, and Zip Code
      2. Select the delivery method for the One-Time Code
      3.  Enter the Verification Code
    4. Create your new password
    5. Confirm new password

  • How do I find my checking account number on a desktop?
    1. Log into Online Banking on desktop
    2. Choose your checking account
    3. Choose Account Details
    4. Scroll to the bottom to view your Checking Number (MICR) and Routing Number

  • How do I register a new online banking account on desktop?

    1. Visit LGEccu.org

    2. Click on Log In → Register

    3. Choose "Register to my individual account" or "Register to my business account"

    4. Read & accept the Online Banking Agreement Disclosure

    5. Confirm your identity with information associated with you account

    6. Verify your identity by receiving a One-Time Password (OTP)

    7. Enter your 6 digit OTP to verify your account

    8. Create your unique username

    9. Create your password

    10. Review and confirm your contact information & time zone

    11. Your account is complete! Start exploring your profile, viewing your accounts, or customizing your settings. 

Internal Transfers — Desktop

  • How do I add an account for internal transfers on desktop?
    1. Under Transfer & Payments
    2. Click on More Actions
    3. Press on Add Account
    4. Under Internal Account, press on Send money to another LGE Community Credit Union member
    5. Enter all required information, Recipient Information – Last Name, Account, Account Type (Share/Loan), Account Number, Share/Loan ID
    6. Click to Save Account for Future Use
    7. Enter Nickname
    8. Click Save
    9. Confirm your identity by Text, Email, or Call, select delivery method
    10. Press Send Code
    11. Enter Code
    12. Click Verify

    An email will be sent to the recipient and to the member adding the account.

  • How do I cancel a scheduled internal transfer on desktop?
    1. Click on Payment Center
    2. Click on Transfer & Payments
    3. Click on the 3 horizontal dots (…)
    4. Select Cancel Transfer
    5. Enter reason for canceling the transfer (optional)
    6. Click Cancel Transfer
    7. Click Done
  • How do I make an internal transfer (one time or recurring) on a desktop?
    1. Visit lgeccu.org, or the mobile app
    2. Enter the username/password
    3. Click on Payment Center/Transfers & Payments
    4. Select the From Account in dropdown
    5. Select the To Account in the dropdown
    6. Enter amount
    7. Select payment frequency (One-Time or Recurring)
    8. Select date from calendar
    9. Enter memo (optional)
    10. Click Review Transfer
    11. Click Submit Transfer
    12. Click Done
    13. View Transfer under Scheduled tab
  • How do I view my internal transfers on desktop?
    1. Click on the Activity tab
    2. Locate the transfer to view
    3. Click on the 3 horizontal dots (…)
    4. Click View Transfer Details
  • How do I edit a scheduled transfer?
    1. Click on the Scheduled Tab
    2. Locate the payment to edit
    3. Click on the 3 horizontal dots
    4. Click Edit Transfer
    5. The member can edit the From/To Account, amount, payment frequency, date and add a memo (optional)
    6. Click Done
    7. View the transfer under the Scheduled tab

Bill Pay — Desktop

  • How do I select multiple Bill Pay payees on desktop?
    1. Select the MultiPay tab
    2. Select payee by scrolling and check each payee box individually
      • To populate all payees, click Add All 
    3. Enter amount
    4. Select Deliver By date
    5. Click Submit Payment
      • To remove specific payees, click the X 
      • To remove all payees, click Remove All
  • How do I add a Bill Pay payee on desktop?
    • Click Add Payee
    • Select Business or Person
    • Fill out the relevant fields
    • Submit
  • How do I delete a Bill Pay payment?
    • Click on the Schedule tab
    • Click on the trash can icon next to the relevant payment 
    • Review and click Yes, Cancel
    • Edit amount, deliver by date, or add memo
    • Click Save
  • How do I edit a scheduled Bill Pay payment?
    1. Click the Schedule tab
    2. Click the pencil icon next to the relevant payment
    3. Edit amount, Deliver By date, or add memo
    4. Click Save
  • How do I schedule a Bill Pay payment on desktop?
    • From the Bill Pay Dashboard, select payee
    • Choose your checking account in the Pay From (Money Markets are not eligible)
    • Enter amount
    • Select payment frequency
    • Select date from calendar
    • The delivery method will display electronic or check payee
      • Electronic Payment – The payment will be sent electronically by ACH. 
      • Check Payments – A physical check is sent to the payee. Schedule payment at least 6 business days to due date.
    • Add memo (optional)
    • Click Submit Payment
    • Click Confirm Payment
  • How do I delete a Bill Pay payee?
    1. From the Bill Pay Dashboard, select payee
    2. Click the Manage tab
    3. Scroll to the bottom of the page
    4. Click Delete Payee
  • How do I access Bill Pay on desktop?
    1. Log into Online Banking
    2. Hover over Payment Center
    3. Click Bill Pay
    4. Review & agree to Bill Pay disclosure

Account Basics — Mobile

  • How do I find my online banking username on mobile?
    1. Open your LGE mobile banking app
    2. Choose Forgot Username or Password?
    3. Click Easily locate your username
      1. Click Find my personal username or business username
      2. Verify Your Identify, enter member number, SSN, DOB, and Zip Code 
      3. Username Recovery Method, select the delivery method for the One-Time Passcode (OPT) by SMS Text, Email or Call
      4. Enter the verification 6-digital OTP code
      5. View your username
  • How do I find my online banking password on mobile?
    1. Open the LGE mobile banking app
    2. Choose Forgot Username or Password? 
      1. Verify your Identify, enter username, SSN, DOB, and Zip Code
      2. Password Reset Method, select the delivery method for the One-Time Code
      3.  Enter the Verification Code, enter the 6-digital OTP code, press Verify, or press on resend code or change method (delivery method)
    3. Enter your new password
    4. Confirm your new password
  • How do I find my checking account number on mobile?
    1. Log into your LGE mobile banking app
    2. Choose your checking account
    3. Go to Show Details 
    4. Scroll down to Checking Number (MICR) and Routing Number
  • How do I delete my online banking user account?

    A member of our Contact Center can handle that for you. You can reach out via secure message by logging into the LGE Mobile Banking  app and tapping More, Tools, and Contact Center; or by logging into Online Banking and clicking Tools, then Secure Message Center.

Internal Transfers — Mobile

  • How do I add an account for internal transfers on mobile?
    1. Press on Transfers & Payments
    2. Press on Add Account
    3. Under Internal Account, press on Send money to another LGE Community Credit Union member
    4. Enter all required information, Recipient Information – Last Name, Account, Account Type (Share/Loan), Account Number, Share/Loan ID
    5. Click to Save Account for Future Use
    6. Enter Nickname
    7. Click Save
    8. Confirm your identity by Text, Email, or Call, select delivery method
    9. Press Send Code
    10. Enter Code
    11. Click Verify
  • How do I cancel a scheduled internal transfer on mobile?
    1. Press on Transfer & Payments
    2. On the Transfer screen under Scheduled Transfer, press on the scheduled transfer to cancel
    3. Press on the scheduled transfer again
    4. Press on Cancel Transfer
    5. Press Done
  • How do I make an internal transfer (one time or recurring) on mobile?
    1. Login to mobile app
    2. Press on Transfer & Payments
    3. Press on Make a Transfer
    4. Select From Account/To Account, enter amount, select frequency, date, and add memo (optional)
    5. Press Review Transfer
    6. Press Submit Transfer
    7. Press Done
  • How do I view my internal transfer history on mobile?
    1. Press on Transfer & Payments
    2. On the Transfer screen under Scheduled Transfer, press on the scheduled transfer to cancel
    3. Press on the scheduled transfer again
    4. Press on Cancel Transfer
    5. Press Done
  • How do I edit a scheduled transfer on mobile?
    1. Press on Transfer & Payments
    2. On the Transfer screen under Scheduled Transfers, press on the scheduled transfer to edit
    3. Press on the scheduled transfer again
    4. Press on Edit Series
    5. Click Save
    6. Click done

Bill Pay — Mobile

  • How do I edit a Bill Pay payee on mobile?
    1. Go to Payment Center
    2. Click Bill Pay
    3. Choose Bill Pay payee to edit
      • If payee is only allowing the account number and nickname to be edited, this means it is a managed payee and the recipient uses a specific address for all payments.
    4. Make edits
    5. Click Save
  • How do I delete a Bill Pay payee on mobile?
    1. On the Bill Pay screen, press on Add and Manage Payees
    2. Select payee
    3. Press on the trash can icon
    4. Press the Delete Payee
    5. Receive Success: Payee Deleted message
  • How do I add a Bill Pay payee on mobile?
    1. Click on Add a Payee
    2. Select Business or Person
    3. Fill out the fields that apply to that Payee
    4. Press Add a Payee
    5. Receive Success: Payee Added message
    6. Review new addition under Payees
