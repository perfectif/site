LGE Community Outreach Foundation

Annually, the LGE Foundation leads a fundraising campaign with the goal of helping give back to the communities we serve. Proceeds raised from our events, as well as personal contributions given throughout the year, are matched* by LGE Community Credit Union and donated to local non-profit organizations, families, or individuals in need. Since 2010, the LGE Foundation has given over $2 million to our communities.

LGE staff members also donate their time by volunteering over 2,600 hours each year!

*Up to an annual maximum.
